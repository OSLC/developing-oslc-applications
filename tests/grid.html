Too many in a row!!

You should see the same behaviors here in all browsers, but they get there different ways.

  • Grade A browsers will have 4 in 1 row, with 1 falling over
  • Firefox (v27 and down) supports the basics of Flexbox, but doesn't support multi-row (flex-wrap) so it must to be lumped into the Grade-C browsers in this case. This is to support the responsive overrides
  • Grade C browsers will have 4 in 1 row, with 1 falling over
